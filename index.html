Bill of Materials

PVC Connectors
  • 4 × 3-way connectors
  • 4 × 3-way elbow connectors
  • 4 × 4-way elbow connectors
¾" Diameter PVC Pipes
  • 6 × 11¾ inch pipes
  • 4 × 17 inch pipes
  • 8 × 7½ inch pipes
Hardware
  • 6 × ¼ inch × 2 inch machine screws
  • 4 × ¼ inch × 1 inch machine screws
  • 8 × ¼ inch wingnuts
  • ¼ inch wooden board
Sifter Trays (17.5 × 11.5 inch)
  • 1 × ¼ in mesh — Soil Sifter, Wide Size
  • 1 × ⅛ in mesh - Soil Sifter, Wide Size
Electrical Components
  • 12 V 10 Ah battery
  • 10 W / 12 V solar panel
  • 12 V vibration motor — 3800 RPM Electric Motor, Vibration
  • DC 12 V–60 V / 20 A motor speed controller

Assembly Steps

Step 1 Building the Top Frame

Attach two 11-inch PVC pipes (D) and two 17-inch PVC pipes (F) by using four 3-way elbow connectors (C). Lightly tap the connectors with a hammer to tighten the frame.

Step 2 Building the Middle Frame

Attach two 11-inch PVC pipes (D) and two 17-inch PVC pipes (F) by using four 4-way elbow connectors (B). Lightly tap the connectors with a hammer to tighten the frame.

Step 3 Connecting the Frames

Connect the top frame and the middle frame using four 7.5-inch connectors (E). Lightly tap the connectors with a hammer to tighten the frame.

Step 4 Attaching the Legs

Attach four more 7.5" PVC pipes (E) to the other side of the 4-way elbow connectors (B).

Step 5 Build the Leg Bases

Build 2 leg bases by attaching two 3-way connectors (A) to 11-inch pipes (D).

Step 6 Attaching the Leg Bases

Attach the leg bases to the legs of the sifter.

Step 7 Cutting the Wood

Cut the wooden plank to 9 × 12 inch size.

Step 8 Attach the Trays to the Frame

Insert the tray with ⅜ in mesh size (G) on the top, and the tray with the ¼ in mesh size (G) on the bottom. Mark the middle of the 11-inch PVC pipe (D) and tray. Drill one hole in each pipe using a ¼ inch drill bit through the tray.

Step 9 Attaching the Motor

Place the motor in the center of the board and mark the four points for the motor attachment. Drill holes using a ¼ inch drill bit at the four points. Attach the motor to the board with the 1-inch screws (I) and wingnuts (J).

Step 10 Attaching the Board to the Sifter

Using 2-inch screws (H), attach the board to the PVC pipes and frames and tighten with wingnuts.

Wiring

Step 11 Wire the Regulator

Attach the wires coming from the battery to the speed regulator’s power terminal. Then attach the wires for the motor to the motor terminal on the regulator.

Step 12 Connect the Battery

Connect the battery to the regulator on the input side. Ensure the positive (red wire) and the negative (black wire) are connected correctly. Similarly, connect the regulator to the motor.
